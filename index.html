About Us

  • Dr. Randeep Singh
  • Dr. Satinder Kaur

MD/DM (Medical Oncology) / ECMO/FCCP

Dr. Singh did his medical graduation from Bharati Vidyapeeth University Pune. Thereafter he received his post graduate training (MD) from Rajasthan University. He has been trained extensively in the management of cancer patients at the prestigious Tata Memorial Hospital (TMH), Mumbai. He did his Doctorate (DM: Medical Oncology) from Tata Memorial Hospital, Mumbai and then worked as a consultant at TMH, Mumbai with the solid tumor-working group before joining Max group of hospitals. He worked as a Consultant with Max Healthcare for five years. Thereafter he was heading the department of Medical Oncology at HCG cancer center, Delhi before joining ARTEMIS Hospitals, Gurgaon.

MD (Obstetrics and Gynecology) from Government Medical College and Hospital, Chandigarh
