Signs and Symptoms

Danger Signs of Cancer

  • Any abnormal lump in the breast OR armpit which is increasing in size gradually
  • Any discharge from the nipple, soreness or retraction of the nipple, different levels of the nipple
  • Puckering of the skin of the breast, dimpling of skin etc
  • Abnormal bleeding in between periods
  • Post menopausal bleeding : Bleeding in females who have attained menopause
  • Post coital bleeding : Bleeding after sexual intercourse
  • Persistent cough , blood tinged sputum, breathlessness
  • Non- healing ulcer in the tongue
  • Hoarseness of voice
  • Difficulty in swallowing food, water
  • Blood in stool
  • Altered bowel habits such as on and off constipation and diarrhoea
  • Loss of appetite and weight loss
