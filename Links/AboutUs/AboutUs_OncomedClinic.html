Oncomed Aim

Our Aim

To prevent and cure cancer through pioneering interdisciplinary research, to translate new knowledge into better prevention and treatment, and to provide effective and compassionate clinical care that improves the lives of patients with cancer and their families.We aim ultimately to increase people’s survival and quality of life by practicing personalized medicine in an ethical manner.

WELCOME TO ONCOMED CLINIC

About Oncomed Clinic

We at ONCOMED clinic aim to provide the highest level of safe, innovative, compassionate care for patients with cancer and their families. You will find experts with extensive experience in the diagnosis and treatment of virtually every kind of cancer and the resources to provide excellent care tailored to their needs. We try to share what we learn with other members of oncology community across the globe with our patients. There is a special concern for the rising Gynecological cancers in the city and we have prominent Gynae oncologist of Delhi NCR (Dr Satinder Kaur) amongst us to address the issues of the women who are suffering from Gynecological cancers.

The clinic is a trusted advisor for an unbiased ethical consult on all oncological treatment, delivered to the highest standards. The patients at ONCOMED clinic shall have peace of mind, enjoy the serenity of the place as we are committed to working with you against this battle.
