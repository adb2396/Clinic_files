Myths and Facts About Cancer

Myths About Cancer

  • If no one has cancer in your family you cannot suffer from it. If you have a family history of cancer, you will surely get cancer
  • If you are diagnosed with cancer, you will probably die in a short time
  • Cancer is always very painful.
  • Older people are not fit for cancer treatment
  • Cancer treatment is usually worse than the disease
  • Ayurvedic/ Alternative medicine treatment/ Herbal products can cure cancer
  • Hair dyes and antiperspirants can cause cancer
  • Artificial sweeteners, flavours, colours and food additives can cause cancer
  • Getting a PET scan in a health individual on regular basis can detect/diagnose cancer early
  • Get rid of your cell phone to avoid brain cancer
  • Dye of hair is not allowed while you are on chemotherapy

Facts About Cancer

  • Cancer can affect anyone. Do not feel guilty. You have done nothing wrong
  • An estimated 5 to 10% of cancers are entirely hereditary. Most cancers develop through a combination of hereditary and environmental factors
  • You cannot catch cancer. It is not infectious
  • Stress/injury to the body does not cause breast cancer
  • 70-80% of women who develop breast cancer do not have any known risk factors or family history
  • There is a vaccination available against virus named HPV and taking this vaccination significantly reduces the chances of cervical cancer
  • Access to cancer treatment in India is available and it is not necessary that it is exorbitantly high
  • Adopting a healthy lifestyle, maintaining a reasonable weight and regular exercise, increasing fiber and reduce fat can help reduce the chances of cancer
  • Screening for Breast (Mammography) / Cervical cancer (PAP) is easily available and is offered to all healthy females to diagnose or detect early changes in body which can lead to cancer
  • Majority of cancer can be prevented by life style modification and screening
